Kent Dagnall is an accomplished media strategist with more than 10 years of experience in every branch of advertising and public relations, especially the realm of digital media and web development. Whether he’s designing creative, producing award-winning TV spots, delicately crafting press releases for touchy subjects, orchestrating attention-getting publicity stunts or conducting multimedia ad campaigns - Kent delivers the right message to the right target at the right time.

A proficient project manager, and a web developer in his own right, Kent is passionate about making sure clients know what’s happening in their campaigns and why it’s working. Getting down and dirty into online analytics, advertising ratings and social media metrics, he provides critical context to unwieldy data and implements changes that consistently outperform expectations.
