    COLUMBUS REGIONAL HEALTH

    ROLE: Chief Developer

    BACKGROUND: Nobody does business exactly like you do. Your competition might offer some of the same stuff, but your company was built on specific values that you strive to communicate to customers and team members every day. How do you communicate these core values, so your business stays healthy, across an organization employing thousands?

    THE IDEA: In 2014, Columbus Regional Health decided to step up their game and make sure that every employee of their hospitals understood the core values that enabled their growth into West Georgia’s largest health care provider. In a proactive move, they started a conversation about the best way to launch an internal social networking website where each employee could deepen their understanding of what it means to be a leader. That’s how the “Simply Better Leadership” network was born...

    THE SOLUTION: At the heart of Simply Better Leadership are Columbus’ core values: Attitude, Commitment, Enthusiasm, Innovation & Teamwork, “ACE-IT” for short. On the website, their entire staff has access to an individual profile with book groups & discussion topics covering the Competencies, Management Skills and Behaviors an ACE-IT leader exhibits.

    We identified and customized the BuddyPress plugin for WordPress as the ultimate social networking solution for sparking dialogue and managing internal communications in a safe, HIPAA-compliant environment. Once the site was launched, Content Creators went to work interviewing Columbus’ employees, discovering inspirational tales from healthcare providers that have helped hundreds of their co-workers unleash leadership potential in their own work. Those of us working on this project have been profoundly affected by the stories we’ve been privileged to help tell, and feedback from the staff of Columbus Regional Health has outshined all our expectations.

    CONFIGURATION & DOCUMENTATION: A social network of any size needs plans in place to efficiently manage user privileges, new user additions, lost credentials and, unfortunately, increasingly sophisticated hacker attacks; and HIPAA or PCI compliance adds a new level of complexity. I've learned when to ask the right questions, and have experience configuring complex IT and can interface with existing IT assets, or launch stand-alone solutions, depending on your needs.

    MANAGED WORDPRESS HOSTING & USER EXPERIENCE (UX/UI) DESIGN: In tandem with Managed WordPress Hosting security specialists, I created a focused user experience within a secure, easily accessible WordPress installation. By building the powerful transactional email service Mandrill by MailChimp into the site itself, user management, newsletters and social network updates all flow into a central analytics that tell Columbus’ management who, how, when and where employees are benefiting from Simply Better Leadership network.
