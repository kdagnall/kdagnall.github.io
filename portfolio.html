    Young Entertainment Mag

    ROLE: WordPress Developer & WordPress SEO

    BACKGROUND: YoungAdultMag.com was a media rich website made up of 5000+ unique pages. The main challenge in rebranding to Young Entertainment Magazine, was that YoungAdultMag.com was built on a custom ASPX infrastructure that does not play well with the PHP-based WordPress database. It was especially important to manually migrate, edit and reorganize past articles in order to preserve their status on the search engine results pages (SERP).

    SOLUTIONS: Each of the following strategies made the new website better, faster, and more responsive to user’s needs and interests. When we are able to avoid complications, improve customer service, simplify work and make everyone a little bit more productive, that's when cost benefits start to accrue and your new website begins to pays for itself.

    MOBILE FIRST RESPONSIVE DESIGN: To anyone with a smartphone in their pocket, it shouldn’t be surprising that websites are accessed more than 50% of the time by mobile users, nor that this number has been growing steadily for years. In spite of this fact, a vast majority of digital messaging is still designed with a desktop monitor in mind and gradually degraded for smaller screen sizes. Flipping that concept on its head, Young Entertainment Magazine’s website was coded with the most vital elements optimized for smaller screens and progressively enhanced with more content as the screen size increases. This means the new site started out fast, clean and lean and always serves only the minimum data that is able to be displayed by whatever device vistiors are using. By following the mobile first philosophy, everyone benefits by saving resources, reducing load times and from higher search engine rankings.

    RAPID PROTOTYPING: No successful product goes to market without first being prototyped and tested. Yet most websites are still launched from a finished graphic design based on the best guesses of the client and designer, and turned over to a programmer to be implemented pixel-by-pixel.

    I cannot stress enough that this is the wrong way to go about things.

    Instead of going straight to Photoshop, we asked several key questions about how real people were using the site. What makes them click in the first place? What pages do they stay and read? What content persuades them to click through to the next page? All these questions need to be answered before you start spending money designing on faulty assumptions.

    During the first week of development, I launched a development mockup of the website based around high quality content and escalated the level of detail within a digital framework. This means no level of abstraction between what you think you’re getting and the reality of your users’ experience.

    URL REDIRECTION: The most important thing to remember about search engines is that they are constantly refining themselves, which means if one or all of the pages on your website disappear for even one minute, you run the risk of being removed from the index. If this happens, you will have to start for scratch completely, as if your brand never existed. To avoid this and other pitfalls, like past users who have bookmarked your pages, you need to map every page on your old site to it’s new location or to another appropriate landing page.

    SEO CONTENT AUDIT: Something content-heavy websites like Young Entertainment need to perform is a content audit, the fairly tedious, but nonetheless essential, process of reading, rating, reorganizing and editing every word on your website. Imagine a giant spreadsheet version of your website and you’re not far off the mark. By far the best time for this is while you are already migrating and rebranding your website.

     

    COLUMBUS REGIONAL HEALTH

    ROLE: Chief Developer

    BACKGROUND: Nobody does business exactly like you do. Your competition might offer some of the same stuff, but your company was built on specific values that you strive to communicate to customers and team members every day. How do you communicate these core values, so your business stays healthy, across an organization employing thousands?

    THE IDEA: In 2014, Columbus Regional Health decided to step up their game and make sure that every employee of their hospitals understood the core values that enabled their growth into West Georgia’s largest health care provider. In a proactive move, they started a conversation about the best way to launch an internal social networking website where each employee could deepen their understanding of what it means to be a leader. That’s how the “Simply Better Leadership” network was born...

    THE SOLUTION: At the heart of Simply Better Leadership are Columbus’ core values: Attitude, Commitment, Enthusiasm, Innovation & Teamwork, “ACE-IT” for short. On the website, their entire staff has access to an individual profile with book groups & discussion topics covering the Competencies, Management Skills and Behaviors an ACE-IT leader exhibits.

    We identified and customized the BuddyPress plugin for WordPress as the ultimate social networking solution for sparking dialogue and managing internal communications in a safe, HIPAA-compliant environment. Once the site was launched, Content Creators went to work interviewing Columbus’ employees, discovering inspirational tales from healthcare providers that have helped hundreds of their co-workers unleash leadership potential in their own work. Those of us working on this project have been profoundly affected by the stories we’ve been privileged to help tell, and feedback from the staff of Columbus Regional Health has outshined all our expectations.

    CONFIGURATION & DOCUMENTATION: A social network of any size needs plans in place to efficiently manage user privileges, new user additions, lost credentials and, unfortunately, increasingly sophisticated hacker attacks; and HIPAA or PCI compliance adds a new level of complexity. I've learned when to ask the right questions, and have experience configuring complex IT and can interface with existing IT assets, or launch stand-alone solutions, depending on your needs.

    MANAGED WORDPRESS HOSTING & USER EXPERIENCE (UX/UI) DESIGN: In tandem with Managed WordPress Hosting security specialists, I created a focused user experience within a secure, easily accessible WordPress installation. By building the powerful transactional email service Mandrill by MailChimp into the site itself, user management, newsletters and social network updates all flow into a central analytics that tell Columbus’ management who, how, when and where employees are benefiting from Simply Better Leadership network.
